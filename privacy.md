# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 23rd April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **SpliceKit** (“the app”) handles information.

---

## Overview

SpliceKit is a free macOS application made available through a public website and GitHub.

SpliceKit is designed to collect as little information as possible. The app does **not** collect, sell, or use personal information for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

---

## Information We Collect

SpliceKit does **not** collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, or payment details through the app.

However, SpliceKit uses **Sentry** to receive crash reports and diagnostic information when the app encounters an error or unexpected failure. This information may include technical data such as:

- Crash Logs
- Device Type
- Operating System Version
- SpliceKit Version
- Diagnostic and Error Data

This information is used only to diagnose problems, fix bugs, and improve the stability of the app. We do not use it to identify you personally.

For more information, please review [Sentry’s Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/).

---

## Analytics, Advertising, and Payments

SpliceKit does **not** use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

SpliceKit does **not** display advertisements.

SpliceKit does **not** include in-app purchases.

---

## GitHub

We use GitHub to host the project website, documentation, source code, and support resources. If you visit our GitHub pages, repository, or submit an issue, your information may be processed by GitHub in accordance with GitHub’s own privacy practices.

For more information, please review [GitHub’s Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Crash and diagnostic data may be processed by Sentry on our behalf for the limited purpose of error monitoring and application stability.

---

## Your Choices

If you do not want crash-related technical information to be sent to Sentry, you should discontinue use of the app or use a version of the app with crash reporting disabled, if available.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

---

## Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/elliotttate/SpliceKit/issues).
